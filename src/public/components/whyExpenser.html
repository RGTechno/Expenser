Why Use Expenser ?

Expenser is a web app that allows you to save your day to day transactions/purchases/expenses etc. into a very Simple Non Complex Form so that you could track your Spendings anytime easily without any hustle

  • Saves Time => No need to find bills to track every expense
  • Analyses Your Transactions in form of Graphs and Charts
  • Builds Automated Budget based on your Spendings
  • You've got yourself a free Accountant
